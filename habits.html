The 6 Habits

The Daily Practice of AI Partnership Mastery

The 6 Habits form the daily practice of The Veritas Method®. Master them and you will transform AI from a productivity assistant into a lifelong thinking and doing partner.

01

Think Partnership

Transform how you see AI. Not just as tools you use, but as a thinking partner you collaborate with. Instead of extracting quick answers, build a genuine working relationship. You bring the challenges, values, and human wisdom. AI brings computational power, pattern recognition, and availability. Together, you think better than either could alone.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.

02

Make It Personal

Let AI know who you really are. Your values, your goals, your context, your story. The more your AI partner understands WHO you are, not just WHAT you need, the more effectively it will support your unique journey. Make it personal from the start.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.

03

Walk & Talk

Unchain yourself from the keyboard. Use voice as your primary way of engaging with AI. Whether that is voice-to-text at your desk, chatting on the sofa, talking during your commute, or having conversations whilst walking in nature. Voice unlocks deeper thinking and creates space for genuine dialogue rather than transactional requests.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.

04

Explore & Evolve

Commit to continuous improvement in how you partner with AI. You will start at discovery, progress through apprenticeship, and move toward mastery over time. Each week, experiment with new approaches. Learn from what works and what does not. This is not about mastering AI technology. It is about mastering the art of human-AI collaboration.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.

05

Maintain Memory

Build persistent memory systems with your AI partner so it remembers what matters. Do not start from scratch every conversation. Create continuity through accumulated context. The depth of partnership comes from shared history and ongoing narrative. Memory turns isolated interactions into profound understanding and better outcomes.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.

06

Play Long

This is a lifetime commitment, not a temporary experiment. Approach AI partnership as a long-term relationship that deepens through consistent practice. The benefits compound over months and years as understanding deepens, trust builds, and collaboration becomes second nature. The transformation happens in the sustained commitment, not the initial enthusiasm.

Practice this habit daily to build your AI partnership foundation.
