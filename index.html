We Don't Have An AI Problem

We have a human adaptation problem. AI is advancing faster than human adaptation. The gap between what the technology can do and how we have learned to work with it is widening every day. That gap is where most of the risk sits. It is also where most of the opportunity sits for those willing to close it deliberately.

A movement, not a product. No sales pressure. No email gates. No barriers. The book is free. The method is open source.
