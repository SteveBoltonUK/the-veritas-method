The 6 Habits

The Daily Practice of AI Partnership Mastery

The 6 Habits form the daily practice of The Veritas Method®. Master them and you will transform AI from a productivity assistant into a lifelong thinking and doing partner.

0 of 6 lessons completed

What You Will Learn

  • How to shift from using AI as a tool to partnering with AI as a thinking collaborator
  • The six daily habits that form the foundation of effective human-AI partnership
  • Practical techniques for deepening AI collaboration through voice, memory, and personalisation
  • How to build a long-term AI partnership practice that compounds over time
01
Think Partnership
▶

Transform how you see AI. Not just as tools you use, but as a thinking partner you collaborate with. Instead of extracting quick answers, build a genuine working relationship. You bring the challenges, values, and human wisdom. AI brings computational power, pattern recognition, and availability. Together, you think better than either could alone.

Key Takeaway: The most powerful AI outcomes come from collaboration, not extraction. Think of AI as a partner, not a servant.

Practice Prompt
I've been treating AI as a search engine. I want to shift to treating it as a thinking partner. Here's a challenge I'm currently facing: [YOUR CHALLENGE]. Instead of just giving me an answer, can we think through this together? Ask me questions, challenge my assumptions, and help me arrive at a better conclusion than I could alone.
02
Make It Personal
▶

Let AI know who you really are. Your values, your goals, your context, your story. The more your AI partner understands WHO you are, not just WHAT you need, the more effectively it will support your unique journey. Make it personal from the start.

Key Takeaway: Generic input produces generic output. The more personal context you share, the more powerful your AI partnership becomes.

Practice Prompt
I want you to really understand who I am so you can support me better. Here's my story: I'm [AGE], I work as [ROLE], and my biggest ambition right now is [AMBITION]. The values that matter most to me are [VALUES]. The thing I'm most proud of is [ACHIEVEMENT]. The thing I struggle with most is [STRUGGLE]. With this context, how would you approach helping me differently?
03
Walk & Talk
▶

Unchain yourself from the keyboard. Use voice as your primary way of engaging with AI. Whether that is voice-to-text at your desk, chatting on the sofa, talking during your commute, or having conversations whilst walking in nature. Voice unlocks deeper thinking and creates space for genuine dialogue rather than transactional requests.

Key Takeaway: Voice frees your mind from the screen and unlocks a more natural, deeper form of AI dialogue.

Practice Prompt
I want to experiment with using voice more in our conversations. What are the best ways to use voice-to-text with AI? Give me a 7-day voice challenge: one simple voice-based AI exercise per day that builds my confidence in talking to AI rather than typing.
04
Explore & Evolve
▶

Commit to continuous improvement in how you partner with AI. You will start at discovery, progress through apprenticeship, and move toward mastery over time. Each week, experiment with new approaches. Learn from what works and what does not. This is not about mastering AI technology. It is about mastering the art of human-AI collaboration.

Key Takeaway: Mastery is a journey of experimentation. Focus on improving the art of collaboration, not memorising AI features.

Practice Prompt
I've been using AI for [TIME PERIOD] now. I want to level up my skills. Here's what I currently use AI for: [CURRENT USES]. What am I missing? What are three experiments I could try this week that would push me into new territory and help me discover capabilities I haven't explored yet?
05
Maintain Memory
▶

Build persistent memory systems with your AI partner so it remembers what matters. Do not start from scratch every conversation. Create continuity through accumulated context. The depth of partnership comes from shared history and ongoing narrative. Memory turns isolated interactions into profound understanding and better outcomes.

Key Takeaway: Continuity is the engine of depth. Build memory systems so every conversation builds on the last.

Practice Prompt
I want to build a memory system with you. Let's start by creating a 'partnership brief' — a document that captures who I am, what I'm working on, my preferences, and our shared context. Ask me the questions you'd need answered to build this brief, and then create it for me.
06
Play Long
▶

This is a lifetime commitment, not a temporary experiment. Approach AI partnership as a long-term relationship that deepens through consistent practice. The benefits compound over months and years as understanding deepens, trust builds, and collaboration becomes second nature. The transformation happens in the sustained commitment, not the initial enthusiasm.

Key Takeaway: AI partnership is a long game. The real transformation comes from sustained commitment, not short bursts of enthusiasm.

Practice Prompt
I want to think about my AI partnership as a long-term investment. Where am I now on my journey (be honest), and what would a realistic 12-month roadmap look like for deepening this partnership? Include milestones, habits to build, and ways to measure whether the partnership is genuinely improving my thinking and outcomes.
