The VERITAS Values

The Ethical Foundation of AI Partnership

The VERITAS Values ensure that collaboration with AI is purposeful, ethical, and impactful. Each value is grounded in rigorous research and time-tested wisdom. Together, they form the ethical backbone of your AI partnership — a set of principles you can install directly into your AI platform to guide every conversation.

Module Progress

What You’ll Learn

  • How to ground your AI partnership in clear, positive values that amplify your best self
  • Why ethical considerations must be integrated from the very outset of any AI interaction
  • How resilience and adaptability turn disruption into opportunity
  • The difference between information and insight — and why depth beats data
  • How to make your AI partnership transformative, authentic, and sustainable over the long term
V
Values-Driven
►

A human-AI partnership grounded in positive values. AI can mirror your intentions and values. Aligning with clear values ensures your AI partner effectively amplifies your best self and core objectives.

Grounded in: The Golden Rule — treat your AI partnership with the same care and intentionality you would bring to any meaningful relationship.

AI becomes a genuine partner, not just a set of tools.

Try This Prompt
"Help me define the core values I want our AI partnership to be grounded in. Ask me questions about what matters most to me in life and work, then help me articulate 3-5 personal values that should guide every conversation we have."
E
Ethical
►

Integrate ethical considerations from the very outset. This ensures that AI usage remains responsible, fair, and beneficial for all stakeholders involved.

Grounded in: Universal Ethics — frameworks that converge on mutual respect, reciprocity, and collective benefit.

Responsible AI usage that builds trust and creates lasting value.

Try This Prompt
"I'm working on [PROJECT/DECISION]. Help me think through the ethical dimensions I might be missing. Who are all the stakeholders affected? What are the potential unintended consequences? How can I ensure this creates benefit, not just for me, but for everyone involved?"
R
Resilient
►

Cultivate the capacity to adapt and flourish amid constant change. This value equips you and your AI partner to navigate challenges and embrace continuous evolution.

Grounded in: Evolutionary Biology — cooperation and adaptability provide survival advantage in changing environments.

You thrive through disruption rather than resist it.

Try This Prompt
"I'm facing a significant change: [DESCRIBE CHANGE]. My instinct is to resist it. Help me reframe this as an opportunity. What would it look like to adapt and flourish through this change rather than fight it?"
I
Insightful
►

Cultivate depth over data. AI partnership should generate profound understanding and practical wisdom, not just information. Seek insights that connect patterns, reveal hidden possibilities, and lead to breakthrough decisions.

Grounded in: Philosophical Wisdom — traditions that prize discernment, understanding, and contextual judgment over mere information.

Transformative insights that lead to superior decisions and meaningful impact.

Try This Prompt
"I have a lot of data and information about [TOPIC], but I feel like I'm missing the deeper insight. Help me move from information to wisdom. What patterns am I not seeing? What's the question beneath the question?"
T
Transformative
►

Leverage AI to generate significant, constructive change for yourself and others. Strive for contributions that elevate, empower, and improve lives beyond your own.

Grounded in: Social Science — trust, cooperation, and reciprocity drive thriving communities and positive-sum outcomes.

AI amplifies your positive impact across all life domains.

Try This Prompt
"I want my work to have more positive impact beyond just my own success. Here's what I'm currently working on: [YOUR WORK]. Help me see how I could make this more transformative — not just productive. How could this elevate or empower others?"
A
Authentic
►

Foster genuine interaction and ensure AI is user-friendly, inclusive, and accessible. Transparency and ease of use are paramount for building trust and achieving profound results.

Grounded in: Neuroscience — empathy and genuine connection are hardwired in humans. Authenticity builds trust in all relationships.

Transparent AI partnership that feels natural and trustworthy.

Try This Prompt
"I want our AI partnership to feel more genuine and less transactional. Give me honest feedback: based on how I typically interact with AI, what am I doing well and where could I be more authentic? What would a more genuine partnership look like?"
S
Sustainable
►

Prioritise long-term viability and lasting impact. Ensure your AI partnership contributes to enduring growth and balance across personal, professional, and environmental dimensions.

Grounded in: Game Theory — reciprocity and long-term thinking succeed in repeated interactions. Short-term exploitation fails.

Compounding benefits that grow stronger over time.

Try This Prompt
"Help me audit my current habits and decisions for long-term sustainability. Here are the main things I'm investing my time and energy in: [LIST]. Which of these are building compounding value, and which might be short-term gains at the expense of my future?"
