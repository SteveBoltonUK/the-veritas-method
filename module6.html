The VERITAS Values

The Ethical Foundation of AI Partnership

Every partnership needs a moral compass. The VERITAS Values provide yours — seven principles that ensure your AI collaboration remains purposeful, ethical, and impactful. Each value is grounded in an established discipline and designed to be installed directly into your AI partnership.

Module Progress

What You’ll Learn

  • The seven VERITAS Values and the established disciplines each is grounded in
  • How to apply each value practically in your AI partnership
  • Why values-driven AI partnership produces better outcomes than unguided use
  • How to install the VERITAS Values directly into your AI platform
01
V — Values-Driven
►

A human-AI partnership grounded in positive values. AI can mirror your intentions and values. Aligning with clear values ensures your AI partner effectively amplifies your best self and core objectives.

Grounded in: The Golden Rule — treat your AI partnership with the same care and intentionality you would bring to any meaningful relationship.

Result: AI becomes a genuine partner, not just a set of tools.

When you ground your partnership in clear values, the AI does not just help you do things. It helps you do the right things.

Try This Prompt
"Help me define the core values I want our partnership grounded in. Ask me questions about what matters most to me, then articulate 3-5 personal values that should guide every conversation we have."
02
E — Ethical
►

Integrate ethical considerations from the very outset. This ensures that AI usage remains responsible, fair, and beneficial for all stakeholders involved.

Grounded in: Universal Ethics — frameworks that converge on mutual respect, reciprocity, and collective benefit.

Result: Responsible AI usage that builds trust and creates lasting value.

Ethics is not a constraint on what you can do with AI. It is the foundation that makes what you do with AI worth doing.

Try This Prompt
"I'm working on [PROJECT]. Help me think through the ethical dimensions I might be missing. Who are all the stakeholders? What are the potential unintended consequences?"
03
R — Resilient
►

Cultivate the capacity to adapt and flourish amid constant change. This value equips you and your AI partner to navigate challenges and embrace continuous evolution.

Grounded in: Evolutionary Biology — cooperation and adaptability provide survival advantage in changing environments.

Result: You thrive through disruption rather than resist it.

Resilience is not about resisting change. It is about adapting so effectively that change becomes your advantage.

Try This Prompt
"I'm facing a significant change: [DESCRIBE]. My instinct is to resist it. Help me reframe this as an opportunity. What would it look like to adapt and flourish rather than fight it?"
04
I — Insightful
►

Cultivate depth over data. AI partnership should generate profound understanding and practical wisdom, not just information. Seek insights that connect patterns, reveal hidden possibilities, and lead to breakthrough decisions.

Grounded in: Philosophical Wisdom — traditions that prize discernment, understanding, and contextual judgment over mere information.

Result: Transformative insights that lead to superior decisions and meaningful impact.

Information tells you what happened. Insight tells you what it means. Wisdom tells you what to do about it.

Try This Prompt
"I have a lot of information about [TOPIC], but I feel like I'm missing the deeper insight. Help me move from information to wisdom. What patterns am I not seeing? What's the question beneath the question?"
05
T — Transformative
►

Leverage AI to generate significant, constructive change for yourself and others. Strive for contributions that elevate, empower, and improve lives beyond your own.

Grounded in: Social Science — trust, cooperation, and reciprocity drive thriving communities and positive-sum outcomes.

Result: AI amplifies your positive impact across all life domains.

The most powerful use of AI is not what it does for you. It is what it enables you to do for others.

Try This Prompt
"I want my work to have more positive impact beyond just my own success. Here's what I'm working on: [YOUR WORK]. Help me see how I could make this more transformative. How could this elevate or empower others?"
06
A — Authentic
►

Foster genuine interaction. Be transparent and honest, even when uncomfortable. Authenticity builds the trust that makes deep partnership possible.

Grounded in: Neuroscience — empathy and genuine connection are hardwired in humans. Authenticity builds trust in all relationships.

Result: Transparent AI partnership that feels natural and trustworthy.

The more honest you are with your AI partner, the more useful it becomes. Authenticity is not a vulnerability. It is a strategic advantage.

Try This Prompt
"I want our partnership to feel more genuine. Give me honest feedback: based on how I typically interact with AI, what am I doing well and where could I be more authentic?"
07
S — Sustainable
►

Prioritise long-term viability and lasting impact. Ensure your AI partnership contributes to enduring growth and balance across personal, professional, and environmental dimensions.

Grounded in: Game Theory — reciprocity and long-term thinking succeed in repeated interactions. Short-term exploitation fails.

Result: Compounding benefits that grow stronger over time.

Sustainable partnerships are not built on what you extract today. They are built on what you invest consistently over time.

Try This Prompt
"Help me audit my current habits for long-term sustainability. Here are the main things I invest time and energy in: [LIST]. Which build compounding value, and which might be short-term gains at the expense of my future?"
