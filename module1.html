Partnership Principles

The Human Commitments

The Veritas Method® begins with you. Before any prompt, any tool, or any technique, there are four human commitments that form the foundation of successful AI partnership. These principles ensure that your partnership with AI is grounded, purposeful, and built to last.

Module Progress

What You’ll Learn

  • How to define your personal “why” for AI partnership and set clear, motivating goals
  • How to accelerate your growth by combining human mentorship with AI partnership
  • Why your environment and community directly shape your AI partnership success
  • How to build a sustainable daily practice rooted in experimentation and a growth mindset
01
Start With Why
►

Progress stems from personal motivation. The Veritas Method® is most effective when it aligns with your individual aspirations. It requires a clear definition of your goals and an honest evaluation of the challenges you seek to overcome.

Key Points

  • Clear identification of your specific goals from your AI partnership
  • Honest assessment of your current challenges and pain points
  • Willingness to make the methodology personally relevant
  • Readiness to invest time to achieve your outcomes
Try This Prompt
"I want to use AI as a genuine thinking partner, not just a search engine. To help you support me effectively, I'm going to share my current situation. My primary goal right now is [YOUR GOAL]. The biggest challenge I'm facing is [YOUR CHALLENGE]. The outcome that would make the biggest difference to my life or work in the next 90 days is [YOUR OUTCOME]. With this in mind, how would you suggest we structure our partnership to give me the best chance of achieving this?"
02
Stand on Shoulders
►

Accelerate progress by leveraging the wisdom of those who have preceded you. This principle recognises that combining human coaching and mentorship with AI partnership accelerates growth exponentially.

Key Points

  • Openness to guidance from human coaches and mentors
  • Willingness to build genuine human and AI partnerships
  • Humility to learn from others’ experiences and mistakes
  • Recognition that both human wisdom and artificial intelligence have value
Try This Prompt
"I've been learning from [MENTOR NAME or BOOK/COURSE] about [TOPIC]. The key insight that resonated most with me was [INSIGHT]. I want to apply this to my specific situation, which is [YOUR SITUATION]. Can you help me bridge the gap between this wisdom and my real-world context? What questions should I be asking, and what would a practical first step look like for someone in my position?"
03
Upgrade Your Circle
►

Intentionally surround yourself with people also committed to growth through AI partnership. Your environment shapes your thinking, your standards, and your rate of progress.

Key Points

  • Active engagement with the Athena Veritas community
  • Willingness to learn from peers’ experiments and breakthroughs
  • Openness to diverse perspectives
  • Commitment to being both a student and a coach
Try This Prompt
"I want to be intentional about who I spend time with and learn from as I develop my AI partnership skills. Currently my closest circle includes [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT CIRCLE - professionally and personally]. Based on my goals of [YOUR GOALS], help me think through: what kind of people and communities would accelerate my growth most? What would I look for, and how might I find or attract them?"
04
Practice Makes Progress
►

Progress and mastery requires embracing experimentation over perfectionism. A growth mindset means viewing setbacks as learning opportunities, staying curious, and adapting based on feedback.

Key Points

  • Willingness to try new approaches even when uncomfortable
  • Viewing setbacks as valuable data not personal inadequacy
  • Commitment to self-reflection and honest assessment
  • Openness to changing your approach based on what you learn
Try This Prompt
"I want to build a consistent daily practice with AI that actually sticks. I've tried before and [DESCRIBE WHAT HAPPENED - did it fizzle out, get inconsistent, feel forced?]. My lifestyle looks like this: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR TYPICAL DAY]. I have roughly [TIME AVAILABLE] to dedicate to this each day. Design me a realistic 30-day AI partnership practice plan that starts small, builds gradually, and has a built-in way to reflect and adjust as I go."
